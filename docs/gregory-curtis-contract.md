## 1. Scope of Services

Aurapath AI ("Provider") will design, build, deploy, and maintain AI agent systems for Gregory Curtis ("Client"), a solo foundation operator focused on environmental impact through political funding decisions and investment evaluation.

### Products

**Product 1 — Political Race Analysis Agent**
- Autonomous monitoring of federal, state, and local political races
- Candidate evaluation against Client's environmental policy criteria
- Data integration with FEC/OpenFEC, ProPublica Congress API, OpenSecrets, polling aggregators
- Weekly actionable funding memos with race assessments, candidate scores, and recommendations
- Funding gap identification and high-impact opportunity alerts

**Product 2 — Digital VC Team Agent**
- Data room ingestion and processing (PDFs, pitch decks, financials, legal documents)
- Three parallel AI analyses per deal:
  - Legal: contracts, terms, regulatory compliance, IP review
  - Financial: projections, valuations, burn rate, cap table analysis
  - Business: market sizing, competitive landscape, team assessment, product-market fit
- Unified due diligence report with risk flags, scores, and investment recommendations
